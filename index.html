Allowances by MP

2008/09 to the first quarter of 2010/11

In June 2009 more than a million documents and receipts were published online. These related to MPs' claims dating back to 2004/05 and up to 2007/08.

In December 2009 these pages were updated to include information about MPs' claims for costs incurred when staying away from their main home in 2008/09 and the first quarter of 2009/10. Expenses from 2008/09 to the general election in May 2010 which were not included in the are published on these pages.

In May 2010 the Independent Parliamentary Standards Authority (IPSA) took over responsibility for paying MP's salaries and processing expenses claims. Further details can be found via http://parliamentarystandards.org.uk/Pages/default.aspx

Allowances by MP

Below is an alphabetical list of current MPs. The Allowances link, to the right of each name, takes you to a page containing edited scans of documents submitted in support of claims made against:

  • Additional Costs Allowance (ACA) - paid to reimburse Members for necessary costs incurred when staying overnight away from their main home in each year from 2008/09.
  • Personal Additional Accommodation Expenditure (PAAE, which replaced the ACA in April 2009) - paid to reimburse Members for necessary costs incurred when staying overnight away from their main home. Please note that the information relates to claims made during April to June 2009.
  • Incidental Expenses Provision (IEP) - the costs associated with running an office in each year from 2008/09.
  • Communications Allowance - introduced in 2008/09, which provides funds for communication with constituents.

Geographical location

A link on the left hand side of the page provides a list of the geographical location of MPs' current ACA/PAAE home - either in London or the constituency. It also indicates if the location changed during 2008/09 or 2009/10, including if that change was within the same geographical area. Additional links provide totals by Member for:

  • Travel costs
  • Stationery and postage costs
  • Details of catering functions and events hosted by Members
  • Repayments made against all claim types
  • Net totals, reflecting the repayments made against the original claims.

Older Expenses Claims

This link provides access to the searchable database of claims from 2004/05 onwards.

Each file is in a PDF format containing details that relate to the year of each allowance. This is shown in the file name. These PDFs are large files and may take time to download.

The scans have been edited to remove information which could cause serious security issues and breach the privacy of the MP, their staff and other third parties. For general information about the allowances and for further information about expenditure, refer to the links on this page. Please note that there have been five editions of the Green Book (the official guidance to MPs on allowances) published from 2004 to 2009 which reflects changes made to the rules between these dates. The links to these editions are on the left hand side of the page.
